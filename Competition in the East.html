Toronto Raptors Starting Lineup

  • Kyle Lowry
  • Danny Green
  • Kawhi Leonard
  • Serge Ibaka
  • Jonas Valanciunas

The Toronto Raptors have been hyped during the regular season. However, when the Raptors reach the post season, they lost momentum. The Raptors are considered a joke in the playoffs to many people. There has been a man for that reason and that man is LeBron James. When he was on the Cleveland Cavaliers, the Raptors and Cavaliers would always meet. During the 2017-2018 season, the Cavaliers swept the Raptors even though the Raptors were the #1 seed in the Eastern Conference. The Cavaliers were fourth. LeBron James has left Cleveland during the 2017-2018 season and went to the Los Angles Lakers. The Los Angles Lakers is a Western Conference team and will not meet the Raptors unless for the NBA Finals. This means the Raptors have a winning chance this upcoming season. The Raptors acquired a big superstar in the league, Kawhi Leonard. The Raptors traded away their arguebly greatest Raptor of all-time, DeMar Derozan. Danny Green has also been traded with Kawhi Leonard. He is a very versatile player as he can shoot and play defense.
